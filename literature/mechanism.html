Generation Mechanism & Technological Evolution

How generative AI turned misinformation from a skilled craft into a one-click, large-scale automated operation.

With the rapid development of generative artificial intelligence technology, the production of false information has changed from a complex process that relies on professional skills to a "one-click" operation that can be completed on a large scale and at low cost by automated tools. The core of this technological evolution lies in the successive breakthroughs and integration of generation adversarial networks (GANs), diffusion models and large language models (LLMs), which fundamentally reshape the production paradigm of synthetic media such as deep forgery and constitute an unprecedented impact on the traditional information ecosystem based on real sources.

01

GANs — Generative Adversarial Networks

The early generation confrontation network systematically realized the synthesis of high-quality images and video content for the first time through the adversarial training of generators and discriminators, laying the technical foundation for deep forgery.

Reference: [6]

02

Diffusion Models

Achieved a qualitative leap in the realism, diversity and stability of generated content through a gradual denoising process, making it almost impossible to distinguish synthetic media from real content visually.

03

Large Language Models (LLMs)

Provides powerful text generation and logical arrangement capabilities for deep forgery — automatically generating highly reliable false news scripts, dialogue content, and driving the intelligent generation of multimodal content such as video from text descriptions.

Key Shift

From "falsified fragments" → to "falsified reality"

This technology integration allows deeply forged creations to expand from a single face replacement to customize the generation of complete characters, scenes, events and narratives — realizing the leap from isolated fakes to fully constructed false realities.[7]

Definition and Category of AIGC

Understanding what Artificial Intelligence Generated Content really is and how it spans across multiple modalities.

Artificial intelligence generated content (AIGC for short) refers to a collection of technologies that use generative AI technology to automatically or assist in creating text, images, audio, video and other content forms.[1] Representative models include the large language model GPT-4 and the image generation model Midjourney, etc.[2] The development of AIGC technology has had a subversive impact on the current content creation form. On the one hand, AIGC has the characteristics of low cost and low threshold in the field of basic content creation, providing the possibility of high-quality content creation for most users.[3] On the other hand, there are also many illegal users who are using AIGC technology to mass-produce false information on a large scale, which affects content ecology and even industry development. It has had a significant negative impact.

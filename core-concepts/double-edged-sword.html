The Double-Edged Sword

Balancing the unparalleled creative potential of AI with its inherent risks and societal impacts.

The increasing maturity of AIGC technology has brought significant benefits to users. For example, AI synthesis anchors have pioneered real-time voice and character animation synthesis in the news field,[5] and can even meet the 24-hour uninterrupted live broadcast; the efficiency of content production has also been greatly improved, meeting the mass production in the era of information explosion.

The Benefits
  • AI synthesis anchors enable real-time voice and character animation in news broadcasting.
  • 24-hour uninterrupted live broadcast becomes possible without human fatigue.
  • Content production efficiency greatly improved to meet mass-production demands.
The Harms
  • "Super real" AIGC content is indistinguishable from reality for a significant portion of the population.
  • Technical illusions cause AI to output content that appears rigorous and scientific but contradicts the facts.


This double-edged sword effect makes us accept and learn AIGC technology while also maintaining due caution and scrutiny.

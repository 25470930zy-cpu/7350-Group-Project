Technical Solutions

Focus on shifting from post-event detection to pre-event traceability, using digital watermarking and C2PA protocol to curb AI misinformation from the source.

Shift from "Detection" to "Traceability"

It is difficult to address the problems of rapid dissemination and forgery of AI-generated content solely through post-event detection. Establishing a pre-event traceability mechanism is the key to enhancing governance efficiency.[18]

Measure 01

Digital Watermarking

Promoting digital watermarking technology, embedding invisible identifiers in AI-generated content to achieve full traceability of the content's source.

Measure 02

C2PA Protocol

Following the Content Authenticity Initiative (C2PA protocol), clearly defining core information such as the producer and generation time of AI content, and establishing a "digital identity card" system to curb the spread of false content from the source.

Social Countermeasures

Building rational cognition and active defense capabilities in the public.

The enhancement of digital literacy is not merely about the popularization of technical knowledge; rather, it involves building a rational cognitive framework for the public regarding AI technology, strengthening their ability to distinguish AI-generated content, predict risks, and develop a sense of responsibility, and achieving a transformation from "passive defense" to "active avoidance".[20]

At the specific measure level, a diversified public education system of "government guidance, university support, media collaboration, and community implementation" should be constructed. Systematic "pre-bunking" education should be carried out:

01

Relying on university research resources, the technical principles and dissemination patterns of AI illusions and Deepfake forgeries should be explained.

02

Leveraging mainstream media and social platforms, through diversified forms such as scenario simulations, case dissections, and expert interpretations, the common generation patterns, misleading logic, and identification methods of AI false information should be revealed to the public in advance.

03

Relying on community education platforms, targeted at the cognitive characteristics of different age groups and different populations, stratified and categorized public education should be conducted, with a focus on enhancing the risk prevention awareness of vulnerable groups such as the elderly and teenagers.
